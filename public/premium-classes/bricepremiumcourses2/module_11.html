Module Overview
B1–B2

Welcome to Module 11: Financial Planning and Strategy. This module develops the professional English you need to engage confidently in corporate financial planning — from building annual budgets and long-range forecasts to presenting strategic plans to senior leadership and boards. You will learn the language of scenario analysis, capital allocation, financial modelling, and performance measurement, and practise the grammar and discourse skills that bring financial strategy communications to life. Whether you are a finance business partner, a strategic analyst, or a CFO preparing for an investor day, this module will equip you to communicate with clarity, authority, and precision.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and correctly use key financial planning and strategy vocabulary in professional contexts
  • Read and interpret formal texts on corporate financial planning cycles, capital allocation, and strategic forecasting
  • Discuss budgeting processes, financial scenarios, and long-term strategies using precise, professional English
  • Understand and produce cleft sentences used to emphasise key financial information in presentations and reports
  • Use future forms accurately — including will, going to, present continuous, and future perfect — to describe plans, forecasts, and projections
  • Apply hedging language to soften claims and express degrees of certainty in financial forecasts and strategy documents
  • Listen to and comprehend spoken English in financial planning meetings, strategy presentations, and analyst briefings
  • Write a formal financial forecast memo and a strategic plan executive summary
  • Analyse real-world corporate financial planning and strategy case studies
