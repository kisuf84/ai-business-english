Module Overview
B1–B2

Welcome to Module 2: Market Research and Segmentation. This module develops the professional English you need to engage with one of the most critical areas of modern marketing practice. You will learn how to talk about research methodologies, interpret consumer data, describe target audiences, and present segmentation strategies with clarity and confidence. Whether you are briefing a research agency, presenting insights to a client, or writing a market analysis report, this module gives you the language tools to do it well in English.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and correctly use key market research and segmentation vocabulary in professional contexts
  • Read and interpret texts about research methodologies, consumer behaviour, and segmentation strategies
  • Discuss target audiences, buyer personas, and market segmentation approaches with confidence
  • Use comparatives and superlatives accurately to compare segments, products, and research findings
  • Apply quantifiers and data language to describe survey results and statistical findings
  • Use the language of contrast and concession to present balanced research reports and recommendations
  • Listen to and comprehend English in research briefings, focus groups, and client presentations
  • Write a market research brief and a consumer segmentation report
  • Analyse real-world market research and segmentation case studies
