Module Overview
B1–B2

Welcome to Module 9: Customer Personas and Storytelling. This module develops the professional English you need to understand customers deeply and communicate with them powerfully. You will learn how to build detailed customer personas that go beyond demographics, how to identify and articulate customer journeys, and how to use storytelling frameworks to create narratives that resonate emotionally. Whether you are writing a brand story, presenting a persona to a client, or using the hero's journey to shape campaign content, this module gives you the vocabulary and language skills to do it with confidence and precision.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and use key customer persona and storytelling vocabulary with precision
  • Read and interpret texts on persona development, customer journey mapping, and narrative strategy
  • Build and present detailed customer personas using professional marketing language
  • Understand and apply tense consistency in narrative and storytelling contexts
  • Use descriptive adjective and adverb patterns to bring personas and brand stories to life
  • Apply discourse markers for sequence and narrative flow in storytelling presentations
  • Listen to and comprehend English in persona workshops, storytelling seminars, and brand narrative sessions
  • Write a customer persona profile and a brand storytelling brief
  • Analyse real-world persona-driven campaigns and storytelling case studies
