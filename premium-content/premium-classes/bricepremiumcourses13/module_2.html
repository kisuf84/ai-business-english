My Favorite Color
▼

📖 Reading Text

My favorite color is green. I love green because it is the color of nature. When I look at trees, grass, and plants, I feel calm and happy. Green reminds me of the park near my house. I often go there on weekends. The park has beautiful green lawns and tall green trees.

I have many green things in my life. My bedroom walls are light green. My favorite jacket is dark green. My school bag is also green. Some of my friends think I have too many green things, but I disagree. I think green is a beautiful and peaceful color.

Green is also my favorite color in food. I love green vegetables like broccoli, spinach, and green beans. My mother makes a delicious green salad. I also love green apples and kiwi fruit. Some people do not like green vegetables, but I think they are very tasty and healthy. Green is a wonderful color in every part of life.

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. What is the writer's favorite color?

2. What does green remind the writer of?

3. What color are the bedroom walls?

4. Which item is NOT green according to the text?

5. Which green vegetables does the writer mention?

6. What green fruit does the writer love?

7. What does green make the writer feel?

8. How do some friends feel about the writer's many green things?

I love elephants because they are very intelligent. They can recognise themselves in a mirror, which very few animals can do. Elephants are unfortunately endangered. Humans hunt them for their ivory tusks, which is illegal but still happens. Their natural habitats are also being destroyed. I think it is very important to protect elephants. I follow several wildlife organisations online that work to save elephants. One day, I would like to visit Africa and see wild elephants in their natural habitat. They are magnificent, gentle giants.
