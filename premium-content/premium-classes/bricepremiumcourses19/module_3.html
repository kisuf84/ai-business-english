Module Overview

Welcome to Module 3: Making a Sales Pitch. Once a lead has been qualified, the salesperson faces one of the most high-stakes moments in the entire sales process — presenting the solution in a way that resonates deeply, addresses the specific needs of the buyer, and creates a compelling case for why they should choose you over all other alternatives. The sales pitch is not a monologue; it is a structured, two-way conversation designed to connect the customer's pain points directly to the value your product or service delivers.

This module builds on the qualification skills from Module 2 and takes you through the full range of pitch formats — from the 60-second elevator pitch and the product demonstration to the formal business case presentation. You will study the language of storytelling, benefit-led selling, objection prevention, and persuasive structure — everything you need to craft and deliver pitches that win business in competitive English-speaking markets.
