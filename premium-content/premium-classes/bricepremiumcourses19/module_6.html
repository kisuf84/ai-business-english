Module Overview

Welcome to Module 6: Upselling and Cross-Selling Techniques. The most cost-effective source of new revenue in any business is almost always existing customers. Research consistently shows that acquiring a new customer costs five to seven times more than growing revenue from someone who already buys from you — and yet many salespeople and customer success professionals spend the majority of their time exclusively on new business, neglecting the vast commercial opportunity that sits within their current customer base.

This module focuses entirely on the language, timing, and techniques of upselling — suggesting a higher-value or upgraded version of what a customer is already buying — and cross-selling — recommending complementary products or services that add value to the existing purchase. Critically, this module treats both techniques not as manipulative sales tactics but as genuine acts of service: helping customers get more value from their relationship with you. You will study the moments that create natural upsell and cross-sell opportunities, the language that makes recommendations feel consultative rather than pushy, and the frameworks for structuring expansion conversations professionally.
