Module Overview

Welcome to Module 7: Customer Retention & Account Management. Winning a new customer is only the beginning. Research consistently shows that retaining an existing customer costs five to seven times less than acquiring a new one, and that the majority of revenue growth in mature businesses comes not from new logos but from expanding and protecting existing accounts. Yet many salespeople focus almost exclusively on the hunt — new prospects, new pitches, new deals — while underinvesting in the relationships that are already generating revenue and that carry the highest potential for long-term growth.

This module covers the complete language of account management — from the onboarding conversation and the quarterly business review (QBR) to the renewal negotiation, the upsell conversation, and the difficult discussion when a client is at risk of churning. You will develop the vocabulary, grammar structures, and conversational frameworks to manage client relationships proactively, communicate value continuously, and position yourself as a trusted partner rather than simply a supplier — in confident, professional English.
