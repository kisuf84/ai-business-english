Module Overview

Welcome to Module 6: HR Policies and Compliance Terms. HR professionals operate within a complex framework of legal obligations, organisational policies, and regulatory requirements. The ability to understand, draft, and communicate policies clearly in professional English is not merely a language skill — it is a legal and organisational necessity. Poorly written policies are unenforceable. Ambiguous compliance language creates risk. Unclear communication of rights and obligations undermines trust and exposes organisations to challenge.

This module covers the complete language of HR policy and compliance: from core employment law terminology and equality legislation vocabulary, through data protection and GDPR language, to the grammar of obligation and prohibition used in policy documents, and the writing skills needed to produce legally sound, professionally clear policy communications. The Writing section features Auto-Correct & Get Feedback — instant, detailed feedback on your grammar, vocabulary, length, and structure.
