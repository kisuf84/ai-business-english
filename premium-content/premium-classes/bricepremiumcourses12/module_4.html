Module Overview

Welcome to Module 4: Training and Development Language. Learning and development sits at the intersection of strategy and people — and it is one of the fastest-evolving areas of HR practice. Whether you are commissioning a leadership development programme, facilitating a training needs analysis, writing individual development plans, coaching a manager on giving constructive feedback, or presenting L&D ROI data to the board, you need a precise, confident, and professional command of the specialist English vocabulary of this field.

This module covers the full landscape of training and development language — the vocabulary of learning design, the grammar of writing measurable learning objectives, the communication skills needed to run a development conversation, and the professional writing conventions for L&D reports, TNA summaries, and individual development plans. You will practise all of these contexts with authentic HR scenarios, real-world case studies, and AI-powered writing feedback that analyses your grammar, vocabulary, structure, and length.
