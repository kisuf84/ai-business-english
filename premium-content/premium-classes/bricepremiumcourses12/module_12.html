Module Overview

Welcome to Module 12: HR Reporting and Documentation. The ability to collect HR data is no longer a differentiator — every organisation with an HR information system can generate reports. What differentiates effective HR professionals is the ability to transform data into insight, and insight into action. This module develops the complete language toolkit for HR reporting: from reading and interpreting HR metrics through writing data commentaries and business cases to the specialist grammar of hedging, discourse markers, and HR reporting collocations that professional HR documentation demands.

This module covers key HR metrics (turnover, absence, time-to-hire, eNPS), the structure of HR reports and business cases, and the grammar of evidence-based professional writing. The Writing section features Auto-Correct & Get Feedback — instant feedback on your grammar, vocabulary, hedging, discourse markers, and structure.
