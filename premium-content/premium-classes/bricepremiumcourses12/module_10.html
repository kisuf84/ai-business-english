Module Overview

Welcome to Module 10: Benefits and Compensation Language. Compensation and benefits sit at the intersection of HR strategy, employment law, and employee psychology. Getting the numbers right is necessary but not sufficient — HR professionals must also communicate compensation clearly, defend pay decisions credibly, conduct benchmarking analysis accurately, and write offer letters and pay equity memos that are both legally sound and professionally compelling.

This module covers the complete language of compensation and benefits: from total reward frameworks and pay equity analysis to benchmarking methodology, salary negotiation, and the specialist grammar of reported speech and condition/concession structures essential for documenting and communicating pay decisions. The Writing section features Auto-Correct & Get Feedback — instant, detailed feedback on your grammar, vocabulary, length, and structure.
