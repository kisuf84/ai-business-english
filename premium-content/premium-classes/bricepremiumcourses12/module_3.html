Module Overview

Welcome to Module 3: Onboarding and Orientation Language. The first days and weeks in a new role have a disproportionate impact on an employee's long-term engagement, productivity, and likelihood of staying. Research by the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) consistently shows that employees who experience a structured, welcoming onboarding process are significantly more likely to still be with the organisation at the 12-month mark — and significantly more likely to feel confident and competent in their role. The language HR professionals use during onboarding is central to that experience.

This module focuses on the full range of English communication skills needed to deliver effective onboarding — from pre-arrival welcome communications and first-day orientation scripts to buddy programme conversations, 30-60-90 day check-ins, and the written documentation that guides new employees through their first months. You will build the vocabulary, grammar, and interpersonal communication skills to make new employees feel genuinely welcomed, clearly informed, and well supported in an English-speaking or international workplace.
