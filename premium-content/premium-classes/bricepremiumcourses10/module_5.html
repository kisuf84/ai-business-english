📋   Grammar Review

Reference Guide

Three reference boxes: tour briefing language (opening and welcome, presenting the itinerary, free time communication, practical instructions, safety and contact); commentary language (setting the scene, bringing history to life, using anecdote and specific detail, inviting the guest to notice, strong closing line); and contingency communication (the five-step framework: state what happened, explain practical impact, present alternative positively, acknowledge briefly, move forward with energy).
