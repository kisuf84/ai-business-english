📋   Grammar Review

Reference Guide

Three reference boxes: describing facilities (atmosphere opener, benefit not feature, sensory language, distinguishing feature, strong closing line); personalised recommendations (linking need to service, qualifying the match, social proof language, specific tonight or this week, soft close); and explaining inclusions and costs (positive inclusion language, package inclusion, stating a charge clearly, treatment charge with inclusion, access instructions).
