Module Overview
B1–B2

Welcome to Module 7: Auditing and Internal Controls. This module equips you with the professional English language skills needed to navigate the world of auditing, compliance, and corporate governance. Whether you are preparing for a financial audit, reviewing internal control systems, or communicating findings to stakeholders, the vocabulary and grammar covered here will help you write and speak with authority and precision in any professional context.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and use key auditing and internal control terminology accurately in professional contexts
  • Read and interpret professional texts on audit procedures, opinions, and compliance frameworks
  • Discuss audit findings, risk assessments, and control frameworks with precision and confidence
  • Understand and produce sentences using passive reporting structures in formal written English
  • Use conditional sentences (Type 2 & 3) to discuss audit scenarios and hypothetical outcomes
  • Apply formal discourse markers to structure professional audit reports and presentations
  • Listen to and comprehend spoken English in auditing, compliance, and governance scenarios
  • Write a professional audit findings memo and an internal control evaluation report
  • Analyse real-world auditing case studies and evaluate the consequences of control failures
