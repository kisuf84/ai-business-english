Module Overview
B1–B2

Welcome to Module 9: Tax and Regulatory Language. This module equips you with the professional English vocabulary and linguistic structures needed to navigate the complex worlds of taxation and financial regulation. From understanding the difference between tax avoidance and tax evasion, to discussing regulatory frameworks such as IFRS, GDPR, and Basel III, this module will help you read, write, and communicate with confidence in English-language tax and compliance environments. Whether you are preparing a tax return, advising clients on compliance obligations, or responding to a regulatory inquiry, the language you develop here will be immediately applicable.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and correctly use key tax and regulatory terminology in professional contexts
  • Read and interpret formal texts about corporate taxation, VAT, transfer pricing, and compliance frameworks
  • Discuss tax obligations, regulatory changes, and compliance strategies using precise, professional English
  • Understand and produce complex noun phrases typical of legal and regulatory writing
  • Use modal verbs accurately to express obligation, prohibition, permission, and advice in compliance contexts
  • Apply formal discourse markers to structure written and spoken tax and regulatory communications
  • Listen to and comprehend spoken English in tax advisory, regulatory briefing, and compliance scenarios
  • Write a formal tax advisory memo and a regulatory compliance summary report
  • Analyse real-world tax dispute and regulatory compliance case studies
