Module Overview
B1–B2

Welcome to Module 6: Investment Terminology. In this module you will develop the professional English vocabulary and linguistic skills needed to discuss investments, financial markets, and portfolio management with confidence. Whether you are reading financial reports, participating in strategy meetings, or advising clients, the language covered here will help you communicate with precision and authority.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and use key investment terminology in professional contexts
  • Read and interpret financial texts about markets, portfolios, and risk
  • Discuss investment strategies and market conditions using precise vocabulary
  • Understand and produce sentences using participle clauses in financial writing
  • Use cause/result linkers to explain financial relationships and outcomes
  • Apply phrasal verbs commonly used in business and investment discourse
  • Listen to and comprehend spoken English in investment-related scenarios
  • Write a professional investment memo and market analysis report
  • Analyse real-world investment case studies and evaluate decisions
