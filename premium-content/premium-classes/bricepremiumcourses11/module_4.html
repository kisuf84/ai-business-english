Medical Records and
Documentation

Master the professional language of hotel services and amenities — from the legal and professional standards governing medical records through SOAP and SBAR documentation formats, discharge summaries, referral letters, clinic letters, and operation notes to the Caldicott Principles, UK GDPR, subject access requests, information governance, clinical audit, records retention, and medicolegal documentation standards. Build the skills to write accurate, structured, and legally compliant clinical records; produce discharge summaries and referral letters that enable safe care transitions; explain information governance principles to colleagues and patients; and communicate about records access, documentation standards, and clinical audit in English.

Real-World Cases
