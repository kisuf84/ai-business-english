📋   Grammar Review

Reference Guide

Three reference boxes: ESG reporting and purpose communication — purpose specificity tests (would we turn down business? does every employee know what it means? can we point to a decision where it cost us?), evidence-before-aspiration reporting structure, and the greenwashing three-part test (specific and verifiable? full scope? backed by credible plan?); ESG stakeholder communication — investor communication distinguishing what you can and cannot demonstrate, social licence versus consultation language (outcome genuinely open versus consultation legitimating a done decision), and supply chain scope acknowledgement; and advanced ESG strategy — impact measurement (outputs/outcomes/impact/attribution), circular economy business model test (does revenue model benefit from extended product life?), and just transition specificity (involuntary redundancy commitment, supplier-switching prohibition, community alternative prerequisite).

Real-World Cases
