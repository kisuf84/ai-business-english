Reference Guide

Three reference boxes: the language of transformational leadership covering vision communication (from goal to meaning), coaching conversation structure (questions over answers), and accountability language (direct, specific, non-blaming); change and stakeholder communication covering the change curve communication stages, stakeholder alignment (interest-based not position-based), and influence without authority; and leadership development and culture communication covering feedback language (specific, behavioural, forward-looking), psychological safety language (creating conditions for honesty), and leadership brand language (consistent values in difficult moments).
